        <h2 class="text-5xl font-bold mb-10">Our Mission</h2>
        <div class="prose prose-invert prose-lg mx-auto text-zinc-300 leading-relaxed">
            <p>
                Beacon Youth Foundation (or Lumina Youth Foundation) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to providing <strong>life-saving ketamine therapy</strong> to adolescents aged 13–17 experiencing acute suicidal ideation, particularly those who have not responded to conventional treatments.
            </p>
            <p>
                We exist to bridge the critical gap in mental health care for teens in crisis by eliminating financial and access barriers to rapid, evidence-based interventions. Suicide is a leading cause of death among young people, and traditional antidepressants often take weeks to work—if they work at all. Our foundation funds and facilitates <strong>medically supervised ketamine therapy</strong> in licensed clinics, delivering <strong>rapid relief</strong> from overwhelming suicidal thoughts—often within hours to days—while integrating follow-up psychotherapy and family support for sustained recovery.
            </p>
            <p class="font-semibold text-teal-400 mt-8 mb-4">Clinical evidence demonstrates the powerful impact of ketamine in this population:</p>
            <ul class="text-left list-disc pl-8 space-y-4">
                <li>Studies show that ketamine can achieve <strong>rapid reductions in suicidal ideation</strong>, with response rates often reaching <strong>55–76%</strong> within 24–72 hours after a single infusion (compared to much lower rates with placebo controls like midazolam).</li>
                <li>In adolescents with treatment-resistant depression and suicidality, single or repeated infusions have led to significant symptom remission, including full resolution of suicidal thoughts in a substantial portion of cases (e.g., up to 63% full remission of suicidal ideation in related trials, and sustained benefits in many with multiple sessions).</li>
                <li>Esketamine (a related form) has similarly shown robust reductions in depressive symptoms and suicidal ideation in teens, with meaningful improvements observed as early as day 6 in randomized trials.</li>
                <li>These effects provide a critical "bridge" to longer-term healing, offering immediate hope when other options fail and helping avert crises that could otherwise lead to emergency interventions or worse outcomes.</li>
            </ul>
            <p class="mt-8">
                Every donation directly funds treatment sessions, clinician oversight, family coordination, and educational outreach—transforming moments of despair into opportunities for healing and a brighter future.
            </p>
            <p class="font-semibold mt-6">
                This foundation is rooted in compassion, urgency, and science: we believe no teen should face hopelessness when rapid, proven interventions like ketamine exist and show strong success in reducing suicidal ideation.
            </p>
            <p class="text-sm text-zinc-500 mt-10 italic">
                *Note: Ketamine therapy for adolescents is typically off-label and must be administered by licensed medical professionals in a supervised setting. This is not medical advice; always consult qualified providers.*
            </p>
